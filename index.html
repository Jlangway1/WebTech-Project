The Leo J Martin Golf Association

"The Leo J Martin Golf Association (LJMGA) is a league of Babson college students who are hungry to play 9 holes day in and day out. We don't care if its during twilight hours, we just love the game of golf."


What is the Average Skill Level of the LJMGA?

Player level ranges from professional (Tyler) to beginner (Wes and Jack). All playing levels are welcome to join the LJMGA. Having your own golf clubs is recommended.
